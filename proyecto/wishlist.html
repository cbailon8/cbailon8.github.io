Wishlist

Games I don't have on my library which I have or have not played before.

DARK SOULS™: REMASTERED

Remastered version of the first Dark Souls, and an absolute masterpiece. I have completed the game at least 3 times, but the replayability it's huge, and what can I say, the pain of Dark Souls is fun. And this version includes the DLC, which it's one of the best segments of the game and the story of this first title.

Current price: 39.99 USD

DARK SOULS™ II: Scholar of the First Sin

Remastered version of the Dark Souls II, which includes all 3 DLCs for this game. This game is... controversial to say the least, because it's really different from the usual Dark Souls. And in a sincere opinion, as much as I hated playing this game, the experience is incredible. It's definitely the worst entry of the series, but still a much better game than current-day releases.

Current price: 39.99 USD

DARK SOULS™ III

The one and only Dark Souls I haven't experienced yet, but another masterpiece. Brought the best from the first title and even adopted elements from the gameplay and lore from the second one. The best way to close a series. This version doesn't include DLCs, they have to be bought separately.

Current price: 59.99 USD

Sekiro™: Shadows Die Twice - GOTY Edition

Another release from FromSoftware, developer of Dark Souls, and another title of the Souls genre. A game with a different gameplay style from the usual Dark Souls, but with mostly the same death penalties. An absolute must play for fans, and I want to play it. It even won the Game of the Year award, so yeah, the public approves.

Current price: 59.99 USD

ELDEN RING

Latest release from developer FromSoftware, and Game of the Year 2022. They surpassed themselves with this Souls-like open-world RPG. It's enough with medieval themes to pick my interest, but this game goes beyond that, picking elements from their other titles like Bloodborne and Sekiro, making their ultimate work and sort of love letter to what they've created.

Current price: 59.99 USD

Half-Life: Alyx

After 15+ years, we didn't get Half-Life 3, but we got something awesome indeed. The good and the bad part of this game is that is a VR experience, but Valve really knows how to push the boundaries of technology with their engines. The most inmersive VR game yet, and a Half-Life title to keep us alive until (hopefully) Half-Life 3 sees the light of day.

Current price: 59.99 USD

Grand Theft Auto V

A classic series of open-world RPG games where you are technically the bad guy? I mean, you play as a criminal, so it's bad? It's still fun anyways, and this last title it's so iconic after almost a decade. I have the game bought on another platform, but Steam has its advantages.

Current price: 19.99 USD

Persona 5 Royal

Persona is a spin-off series from the Shin Megami Tensei games. I hadn't seen a single Persona title before this one, but it caught my attention. Even if the art style isn't particularly my favorite, it's still pretty cool. And the OSTs are absolutely bangers. I'm sure this will be a very good introduction to this series. And this games touch topics that are actually really sensitive IRL, so it would really be an experience.

Current price: 59.99 USD
